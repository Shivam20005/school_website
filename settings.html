London (Airstrip One)

Airstrip One is a province. It includes what used to be London, where Winston Smith lives. It is part of the larger superstate, Oceania. The latter is made up of North and South America, the Atlantic islands, Australasia, and southern Africa. (Book Analysis)

"WAR IS PEACE. FREEDOM IS SLAVERY. IGNORANCE IS STRENGTH."

"Big Brother is watching you."

Room 101

Room 101 is a prison chamber in 1984 in which a prisoner's worst fear is manifested, as determined by the Party. The Party, through their various means of investigating and spying on their citizens, is all-knowing (as Winston is reminded of when he's arrested.) They are aware of every citizen's worst fear and are prepared to use that fear against them. (Book Analysis)

"The thing that is in Room 101 is the worst thing in the world."
